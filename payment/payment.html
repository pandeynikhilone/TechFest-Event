Code Golf

  • A speed coding competition where efficiency is key.
  • Only solo participation is allowed.
  • Each participant will have to choose one program out of two.
  • Questions given on the day of the competition and type the code within an hour.
  • The program with the least use of characters will be declared as the winner.
  • The program should be working with no errors
  • Computer system will be provided by the college itself.
  • Registration fee: Rs. 50 per participant.
  • Participants can choose their preferred.
  • Programming language eg: C, C++, Java, Python.
  • Use of any external hardware or storage device will lead to disqualification.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding to all.
  • All Participants to bring their identity proof [ aadhar card + College id card ] for validation.
  • Late comers will be disqualified.
